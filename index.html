The Challenge in North East India

The "Organic Hub" of India faces unique hurdles. Hilly terrain, shifting cultivation (Jhum), and remote valleys create a disconnect between modern agritech and the farmer on the ground.

Technical Architecture

How we achieve high precision with low cost.

The Calibration Problem

Satellite data (Sentinel-2) gives a macro view (NDVI) but is often blocked by clouds in NE India. Ground sensors are accurate but expensive to deploy everywhere.

Our Solution: Fusion Model

  • 1. **Village Stick** takes 1 precise reading.
  • 2. **AI Model** correlates this with available clear satellite pixels.
  • 3. **Result:** Accurately "predicts" soil moisture for the whole village.
